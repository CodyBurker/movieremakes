Aquamarine is a 2006 American-Australian teen fantasy romantic comedy film directed by Elizabeth Allen, loosely based on the 2001 young adult novel of the same name by Alice Hoffman. It stars Emma Roberts, Joanna “JoJo” Levesque in her film debut and Sara Paxton as the eponymous character. The film was released in the United States on March 3, 2006, by 20th Century Fox.
