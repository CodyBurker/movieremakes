Spirit Untamed is a 2021 American animated adventure film produced by DreamWorks Animation and distributed by Universal Pictures. The film was directed by Elaine Bogan and co-directed by Ennio Torresan Jr., from a screenplay written by Aury Wallington and Kristin Hahn. It is the second theatrical film release of the Spirit franchise and is a reboot of the traditionally animated Academy Award nominated Spirit: Stallion of the Cimarron (2002) and based on the characters from its Netflix animated spin-off television series Spirit Riding Free, developed by Wallington.
