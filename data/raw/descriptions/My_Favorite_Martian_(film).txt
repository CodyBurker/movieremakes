My Favorite Martian is a 1999 American science fiction comedy film directed by Donald Petrie. It is based on the 1960s television series of the same name which Ray Walston starred in. The film stars Christopher Lloyd, Jeff Daniels, Daryl Hannah, Elizabeth Hurley, Wallace Shawn, Wayne Knight and Walston. A Mickey Mouse Works short, Pluto Gets The Paper: Spaceship, was shown in theaters with it.
