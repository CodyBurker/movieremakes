Journey to the Center of the Earth is a 2008 American 3D science fantasy action-adventure film directed by Eric Brevig and starring Brendan Fraser in the main role, Josh Hutcherson, and Anita Briem. Produced by New Line Cinema, it is an adaptation of Jules Verne's 1864 novel and was released in 3D theaters by Warner Bros. Pictures on July 11, 2008 where it tells the story of a volcanologist and his nephew who to go look for his missing brother with help from an icelandic guide as they come across the center of the Earth.
