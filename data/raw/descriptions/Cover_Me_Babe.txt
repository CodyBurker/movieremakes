Cover Me Babe is a 1970 drama film about a young filmmaker who is hypercritical about everything including his own work. Almost really doesn't care to get a studio contract. The film was directed by Noel Black, and stars Robert Forster and Sondra Locke.
The title song was written by Fred Karlin and Randy Newman, and performed by Bread. A second song by Bread also appeared on the soundtrack.
