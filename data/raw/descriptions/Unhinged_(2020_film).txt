Unhinged is a 2020 American action thriller film directed by Derrick Borte, from a screenplay by Carl Ellsworth. The film stars Russell Crowe, Caren Pistorius, Gabriel Bateman, Jimmi Simpson and Austin P. McKenzie. It tells the story of a woman who is threatened by a seemingly mentally ill, violent and revenge-obsessed stranger, following a road rage incident at a traffic light, which leads to the stranger targeting and tracking down the woman's family and friends as revenge.
