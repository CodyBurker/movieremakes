My Cousin Vinny is a 1992 American legal comedy film directed by Jonathan Lynn, written by Dale Launer, produced by Launer and Paul Schiff, and starring Joe Pesci, Ralph Macchio, and Marisa Tomei alongside Mitchell Whitfield, Lane Smith, Bruce McGill, and Fred Gwynne, in his final movie appearance. The film was distributed by 20th Century Fox and released on March 13, 1992.
