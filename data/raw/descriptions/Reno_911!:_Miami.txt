Reno 911!: Miami is a 2007 American cop comedy film based on Comedy Central's Reno 911! and directed by series co-creator Robert Ben Garant. Distributed in the United States and Canada by 20th Century Fox and internationally by Paramount Pictures alongside Comedy Central Films, the film was theatrically released on February 23, 2007, receiving mixed to negative reviews, with much of the criticism aimed at its lack of appeal to non-fans.
