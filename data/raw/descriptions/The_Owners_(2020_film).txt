The Owners is a 2020 horror thriller film adapted from the graphic novel Une nuit de pleine lune by Hermann Huppen and Yves H. Directed and co-written by Julius Berg in his feature film directorial debut, the film stars Maisie Williams, Sylvester McCoy, Rita Tushingham, Jake Curran, Ian Kenny and Andrew Ellis. The film is set in 1990s and is about a retired married couple in rural England whose home is besieged by four young criminals.
