The Cooler is a 2003 American crime drama film directed by Wayne Kramer, from a screenplay by Frank Hannah and Kramer. The film stars William H. Macy, Maria Bello, Shawn Hatosy, Ron Livingston, Estella Warren, Paul Sorvino, and Alec Baldwin. It is a true story based on Greek punter Nick Pagalis. It follows a casino "cooler" whose mere presence at the gambling tables usually results in a streak of bad luck for the other players and people in his betting office to whom provides lunch and light refreshments, and the occasional scotch.
