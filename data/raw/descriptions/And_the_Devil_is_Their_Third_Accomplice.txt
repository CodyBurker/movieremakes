And the Devil is their third accomplice is a 1978 psychological drama romance film directed by Sami Shalaby and based on a scenario of the same name written by Youssef Francis. The film stars Mervat Amin and Mahmoud Yassin as a young couple who fall in love. The story is narrated from the present day by Hesham, telling the tale of how he ended up in prison after being accused of the kidnapping of Afaf, to the audience.
