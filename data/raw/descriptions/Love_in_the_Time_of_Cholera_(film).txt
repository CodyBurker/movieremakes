Love in the Time of Cholera is a 2007 American romantic drama film directed by Mike Newell. Based on the 1985 novel of the same name by the Colombian Nobel Prize-winning author Gabriel García Márquez, it tells the story of love between Fermina Daza and her lover, Florentino Ariza and her husband Juvenal Urbino which spans 50 years, from 1880 to 1930.
