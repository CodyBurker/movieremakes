Lost Girls & Love Hotels is a 2020 American erotic drama film directed by William Olsson from a screenplay by Catherine Hanrahan, based on Hanrahan's 2006 novel Lost Girls and Love Hotels. The film stars Alexandra Daddario as an American English teacher in Tokyo, who loses herself to the city's nightlife and begins an affair with a member of the Yakuza gangs. It was released through video on demand on September 18, 2020, by Astrakan Film AB.
